# Content Engine (cross-vertical adaptation layer)

The engine is the proven house canon, not a generic rebuild. This file is the per-vertical adapter.

## Video structure (house formula)
Primary hook (concept, no payoff) → Supporting hook (tension) → Context (why it matters) → Re-hook (new tension before reveal) → Payoff (HELD TO LAST SECOND) + loop trick.
**House standard:** hook lands by **1.7s**, total **21–34s**, swipe-rate target **>78%**, reading level 5th–8th grade, evergreen language.

## Retention rules
Delay payoff to last second; first 2–3s is the only metric that matters initially; **sentence removal not rewrite** when retention dips; analyse the retention graph 1–2 days post-publish and cut the pacing-error segment from all future scripts; incongruous first frame forces attention; per-sentence VO tonality. Saves ← keepable lists/steps; shares ← social currency / "this is so me" / high-arousal; comments ← mild controversy / fill-in-the-blank.

## Hook formulas (swap the noun per vertical)
Curiosity · Fear · Mistake · Contrarian · Before/After · Status · Money · Lifestyle · Identity · Pain-point · Secret reveal · Case study · "I tried X so you don't have to" · "Most people get X wrong" · "If you're [audience], watch this" · "I wish I knew this earlier" · "Stop doing this" · "This is why you're not getting results".

Worked example (swap last noun):
- Investment: "If you're 35 and everything's still in your savings account, watch this."
- Interior design: "Stop choosing your renovation contractor like this."
- SaaS: "Most founders pick their CRM wrong — here's the 1 question that matters."
- Personal brand: "I rewrote my LinkedIn headline and got 3 interviews — here's the formula."

## Pillars + cadence
Pillars: Authority/education · Trust/proof · Personality/founder · Offer/lead-gen (~40/30/20/10; offer never >~20%). Cadence: TikTok/Reels 4–7×/wk, LinkedIn 2–3 deep/wk, Shorts for evergreen/search.

Finance scripts must be **education, not advice**, with disclaimers + FI pre-approval — see `04-finance-compliance.md`.
